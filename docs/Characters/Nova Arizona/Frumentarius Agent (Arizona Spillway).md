## Bio:

The Frumentarius Agent is an unnamed [[Frumentarii|Frumentarius]] camping above the [[Arizona Spillway]] in 2281. He is performing reconnaissance on the [[New California Republic|NCR]]'s defenses at [[Hoover Dam]].
